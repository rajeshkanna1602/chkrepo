Pioneer Wincon

Pioneer Wincon is one of the leading wind turbine manufacturers in India. Belonging to Pioneer Asia Group, PioneerWincon is the market leader in 250KW and 750KW segment. The company has installed about 1000 nos. of turbines across the country in various states. The assembly facility is in Puducherry and the Corporate and Registered Office is in Chennai. Pioneer Wincon (A Unit of Para Enterprises Pvt Ltd) is an ISO 9001:2008 Certified company. The Company has a wide customer base and has a dedicated service division with more than 128 skilled employees deployed in all site offices. Pioneer Wincon, a member company of the Pioneer Asia Group, India has over the last decade become a trusted name and an emerging market leader in wind energy. The core business consists of design, manufacture, sales, marketing and maintenance of wind power systems that harness the energy of wind to generate electricity. The Pioneer Asia Group, which today boasts a group turnover of over Rs. 5 Bn was also the first in India to install a private wind farm connected to the TNEB grid as early as 1989. Pioneer Wincon was conceived as a joint venture between the Pioneer Asia Group and Wincon West Wind of Denmark well known for their turbine generator's simple, sturdy design and advanced power electronics optimizing it for Indian grid conditions. Following the exit of the joint venture partners in 2003, Pioneer Wincon is now a wholly owned Indian company with the design rights and the ability to manufacture and market the turbine worldwide. Pioneer Wincon has corporate office in Chennai and manufacturing unit at Pondicherry. They have installations in Tamil Nadu, Andra Pradesh, Karnataka, Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh and are ready to make marketing in-roads into Gujarat and Rajasthan. 1000+ turbines installed all over the country.
